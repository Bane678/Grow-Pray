🌿 Grow Pray — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

Grow Pray ("we", "us", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Location Data: We request your device location solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Your coordinates are processed on-device and are never sent to any server.

Prayer & Progress Data: Your prayer completions, streaks, coins, garden progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Purchase Data: If you subscribe to Premium or purchase coins, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple/Google. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Notifications: If you grant notification permission, the app schedules local push notifications on your device to remind you of prayer times. No notification data is sent to any server — all scheduling happens entirely on-device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not have user accounts or login systems

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. There is no cloud backup or sync in this version.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Aladhan API — to fetch prayer time calculations based on your coordinates. Only your latitude and longitude are sent to this service. See aladhan.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

5. Children's Privacy

Grow Pray does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date.
