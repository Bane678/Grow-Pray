Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How are prayer times calculated?

Prayer times are calculated based on your device location using the Aladhan prayer time API. Ensure location access is enabled for accurate times.

Q: What happens if I miss a prayer?

If a prayer window passes without completion, the tree for that prayer slot will wither. You can claim dead trees and plant new ones from the Garden Shop.

Q: How do streak freezes work?

A streak freeze protects your streaks for one day if you miss prayers. Purchase them from the Garden Shop using coins. They activate automatically when needed.

Q: What is Difficult Day mode?

Having a tough day? Difficult Day mode reduces prayer requirements with compassion. You get 3 uses per month — because everyone needs grace sometimes.

Q: How do I restore my Premium subscription?

Go to Settings (gear icon) → tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID or Google account.

Q: Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. Cloud backup is planned for a future update. Please do not delete the app if you want to keep your progress.

Q: How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Grow Pray → Cancel
Android: Play Store → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions → Grow Pray → Cancel
